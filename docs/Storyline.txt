Mystery-

The Hidden Will — Escape Room Story
A rich, wealthy man left his will to his two daughters, but not his son.

You — the son — have always been smarter than your sisters and share the same keen intellect your father was known for. But something about the will doesn’t seem right. It’s too simple, too final. You’re convinced your father left you something else — a secret inheritance hidden behind clues only you could solve.

At your father’s funeral, one of the doves you and your sisters released has a small tag around its leg with the numbers 9164 engraved into it.

The next day, after the viewing, you explore your father’s study and find a wooden chest with a padlock. Instinctively, you enter the numbers 9164 — the lock clicks open.

Inside, you find a ring full of keys. Later that night, as you lie awake, you remember being a child — your father once showed you those very keys, whispering:

“They lead to a silent place, but only when you’re ready to listen.”

The next morning, you return to your father’s office. On his desk lies a dusty blueprint of the mansion.

1. The Blueprint
You spread the blueprint across the desk. Faded ink circles mark several rooms — but one, labeled “Silence Room,” has been crossed out.

2. The Hidden Message 
Scrawled faintly in the corner, you read:
“Only one who listens can find the truth beneath the noise.”

3. The Envelope
Beneath the blueprint, you find a sealed envelope with your family crest. Inside is a burnt note:
“The lies they tell will guide you to the truth.”

4. The Compass Key
A small brass compass key falls out of the envelope. When placed on the blueprint’s north marking, it points toward the east wing, an area said to be sealed decades ago.

5. The Locked Drawer
Behind your father’s desk, a hidden drawer refuses to open. Carved into the wood are strange runes — a code your father once taught you.

6. The Music Box
Inside the drawer lies a tiny music box that plays four broken notes: 9, 1, 6, 4.

7. The Family Portrait
The tune stops, revealing a slip of paper:
“The answer lies where memories never fade.”

8. The Truth Lock
Touching the corner of the portrait releases a latch, revealing a small safe with a plaque:
“Truth unlocks the final gate.”

9. The Ring of Keys
Inside the safe is the same ring of keys you found before, each engraved with a single Latin word. One key reads “Veritas Vincit” — “Truth Prevails.”

10. The Hidden Will
You insert the “Veritas Vincit” key into a small keyhole behind the portrait. The wall creaks open, revealing a velvet box. Inside lies the true will and a handwritten letter:

“My son — you have proven that intelligence guided by honesty is the greatest inheritance of all.”
